More about the author

Cornelius Griep is currently conducting research within the working group 'Diversity and Hybridity in the context of Culture, Language and Communication' of the Technical University of Berlin. In his free time he is promoting intercultural dialogue, for example, by organizing study tours to Africa and Latin America.
